LITERATURE REVIEW

According to the data found in the literature review, an application has already been developed to warn individuals and prevent the spread of the disease by using IOT, which contain cloud fag commuting sensors to identify the dengue disease. While observing that study and got an idea to develop a new product for the COVID-19 disease and created a mobile application that helps to work effectively in a limited time. In this article, the portable physiological checking framework will check the patient’s heart rate, temperature, blood oxygen level and blood pressure. However, those research components do not have the functionality to analyze both factors at the same time and make a forecast about the infection of COVID-19 using a Trained ML Algorithm. To predict infected suspects, the approach employs RMI images or chest X-ray data. The suggested method uses a Deep Learning model to detect infected lung X-Ray images. It is based on a modified Google Net pre-trained model. The Confusion Matrix and the ROC curve are used to assess the predicted accuracy. This type of research will aid in the detection of COVID-19 infected individuals and the reduction of the disease, which is a threat to human life. Due to the length of time it takes to obtain a patient’s lung X-Ray, it is almost difficult to determine whether a patient has contracted COVID-19 in a short period of time.
