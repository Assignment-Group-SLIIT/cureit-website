What is CUREit?

COVID-19 Trcaer is a platform design to provide protection and health care for citizens and residents referred to domestic isolation or quarantine:to ensure their safety and enhance their recovery procedures.



The objective of this work is to develop a CUREit that is capable of corona detection and mitigation. The goal of this research is to reduce the number of COVID-19-related fatalities in Sri Lanka while also enabling users who are afflicted with the disease to access appropriate care and hospitalization. This software uses digital technologies to acquire accurate data and provide precise interpretations based on that data. Using every preventative measure available, this mobile application has now been developed to safeguard against the COVID-19 disease.

Features of Our System

There are six major components in our System

covid self-assessment feature

The mobile app prompts a scientific questionnaire to analyze symptoms and calculates the potential of having COVID-19 to that person before doing a PCR test

Safe to go Feature

This module helpful for users before going to a particular place whether it is risk free or not.

Online video consultation

This module provides users to contact consultants via a trivial video consultation system to get further mental assistance.

Potential cluster prediction

Act as a risk analysis component (risk model) since it will proactively detect and analyze areas which can be highly impacted.

Social distance maintenance feature

Alert users when they are nearby a person who has a high potential of having COVID-19.

Conversational AI bot to assist mental conditions

Conversational AI BOT which can imitate human thinking can approach isolated, quarantined patients and improve their mental health and wellbeing.
