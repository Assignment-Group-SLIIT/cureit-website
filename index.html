What is CUREit?

CURE-IT is a revolutionary application specifically developed to cater to the unique needs of dementia patients and their caregivers in Sri Lanka. Our mission is to empower individuals living with dementia by offering music, physical, and game-based therapies that help control and manage the progression of the condition. With CURE-IT, you can embark on a transformative journey towards enhanced well-being and track your progress every step of the way.



CURE-IT aims to develop and maintain an innovative application catering to the unique needs of dementia patients and caregivers in Sri Lanka. The application will provide music, physical, and game-based therapies to control and manage the progression of dementia. Key components include developing a user-friendly application, integrating therapy modules, implementing progress tracking, providing user support, localizing content, ensuring security and privacy, and collaborating with healthcare professionals. The scope focuses on enhancing well-being and empowering individuals through a transformative journey with the ability to track progress.

CUREit Focus on

Music Therapy

CURE-IT offers personalized music therapy sessions to stimulate cognitive functions, evoke positive emotions, and reduce anxiety, ultimately enhancing the overall well-being of dementia patients.

Physical Therapy

Through guided exercises and routines, CURE-IT promotes physical activity to improve mobility, flexibility, and strength, ensuring a holistic approach to dementia care.

Game-Based Therapy

CURE-IT provides interactive games and puzzles that challenge and stimulate cognitive skills, helping to enhance memory, attention, and problem-solving abilities.

Progress Tracking

CURE-IT includes comprehensive tracking tools to monitor the effectiveness of therapy sessions, allowing users to record completed activities and track improvements over time.

User Support

Analyze CURE-IT offers a robust support system within the application, assisting users in navigating the platform, accessing therapies, and resolving any technical issues they may encounter.

Localization and Collaboration

CURE-IT ensures that the application is culturally and linguistically tailored to the specific needs of dementia patients and caregivers in Sri Lanka. It also collaborates with healthcare professionals and organizations to align therapies with best practices and the latest research in dementia care.

Features of Our System

There are six major components in our System

MMSE Test

The system includes the capability to conduct the Mini-Mental State Examination (MMSE) test, a widely used screening tool to assess cognitive function in individuals with dementia. This feature helps track and measure cognitive abilities over time.

Music Playlist Creation

Users are automatically suggested with personalized music playlists within the application. This feature allows caregivers and patients to select and organize preferred songs, promoting a therapeutic environment and evoking positive emotions.

Schedule Creation with Reminders

CURE-IT enables the creation of customized schedules for therapy sessions, appointments, and medication reminders. Users can set reminders to ensure timely engagement in activities and therapies, facilitating consistent care.

Physical Activity Guidance with Video and Audio

The system provides guidance for physical activities through instructional videos and audio prompts. Users can access a range of exercises tailored to their abilities, enhancing mobility, flexibility, and strength.

Countdown Timers for Time Allocation

Alert CURE-IT includes countdown timers to help users allocate and manage time effectively during therapy sessions. This feature assists in maintaining a balanced routine and ensures optimal engagement in different activities.

Game Level Suggestions and Automatic Physical Activity Suggestions

The application offers game level suggestions based on users' cognitive abilities, ensuring appropriate challenge and stimulation. Additionally, CURE-IT provides automatic physical activity suggestions based on the user's progress and preferences, promoting an active lifestyle.

Track Progress

The system tracks users' progress by recording completed activities, measuring performance in therapy sessions, and providing detailed progress reports. This feature offers insights into the effectiveness of therapies and allows for personalized goal setting and continuous improvement.
